About COFAB Pty Ltd

A Trusted Name in Steel Fabrication and Construction

COFAB Pty Ltd is a proudly Australian-owned company led by Patrick Senior, who brings over 26 years of experience in the steel fabrication and construction industry. Since our beginnings, we’ve built a solid reputation for delivering quality workmanship, dependable service, and tailored steel solutions for projects of all sizes.

Our workshop is equipped with advanced tools and machinery, allowing us to take on custom fabrication, welding, and structural projects across residential, commercial, and industrial sectors. From concept to completion, our team ensures that every job meets the highest standards of safety, precision, and durability.

What We Do

  • Structural Steel & General Fabrication
  • Sheet Metal & Aluminium Work
  • Stainless Steel Fabrication
  • On-Site Welding & Installation
  • Steel Rolling, Pipe Work & Cutting
  • Pressure Vessels, Air Receivers, Vacuum Tanks & Boilers
