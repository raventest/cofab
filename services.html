Our Services

Building with Strength. Delivering with Experience.

Structural and General Fabrication

We specialise in the manufacture and installation of structural steel and general fabrication work for projects of all sizes — from small residential structures to large-scale industrial installations. Our precise craftsmanship ensures structural reliability and long-term durability.

Installation and Erection of Steel Work

Our professional team offers full on-site installation and steel erection services, following safety and engineering standards to ensure a seamless build from delivery to completion.

Steel Supplies

As a trusted Australian-owned steel supplier and fabricator, we provide high-quality steel materials, cut-to-size components, and full fabrication solutions for clients in construction and manufacturing.

On-site Welding

We bring the workshop to your site. Our certified welders handle repairs, modifications, and custom welding work directly on-site with precision and speed.

Pressure Vessels, Air Receivers, Vacuum Tanks & Boilers

COFAB has extensive experience in manufacturing and maintaining pressure-rated equipment. All our vessels and tanks are fabricated to meet strict quality, performance, and safety standards.
